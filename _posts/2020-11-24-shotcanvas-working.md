# shotcanvas

shotcanvas is a Puredata plugin. This plugin is designed to use a PNG image as background of a pd patch. You can also create a PNG snapshot of your patch.

tested on linux (Ubuntu 20.10) with tcl Tk 8.6 and pd (vanilla) 0.51.1

github: [shocanvas](https://github.com/marrongiallo/shotcanvas)
